A War Thunder-inspired bodyweight fitness tracker designed to build a highly aesthetic, V-taper physique.

The Doctrine (How to Train)

To get that wide-shouldered look, you need total workout volume, but the Tech Tree only cares about your raw, unbroken power. Here is how you execute the strategy:

The Tech Tree Slider (Your Max Unbroken Set): The tracker logs your absolute limit in one go. If the goal is 30 reps and your muscles give out at 15, you slide it to 15. You only unlock the next rank when you can hit the target number in one single, perfect set without stopping.

The Workout Volume (Building the Muscle): Just because you tapped out at 15 for the tracker doesn't mean the workout is over. To actually grow the muscle, you must finish the total reps. Take a 60-second rest, do 10 more. Rest again, do the last 5.

The Rule: Grind the total volume in smaller sets to build the muscle, but push your max unbroken sets to unlock the next tier.
